Ageless Skin

Ageless Skin

Soon some members of the skin will be able to escape the toll of time on their skin. Wrinkles will form over a much longer time than normal due to a genetic enhancement taken from the lobster which enables constant regeneration of new, young skin.

Mood-Colour Iris

Mood-Colour Iris

The DNA of South American jelly fish has been synthesised into liquid which interacts with the microbiology of the iris resulting in a shifting eye colour dependant on the mood of the host.

Limb Regeneration

Limb Regeneration

Just like a lizard’s tail regrowing, bones to muscle to skin, this trait has been found to lay dormant in some humans and can be activated when stimulated by an injection of the modified spinal fluid of the common penny lizard.

Super Strength

Super Strength

By taking the structures of dolphin musculatory systems, humans can be enhanced to improve muscle use efficiency and strength by up to 130% without any additional muscle being built.

Eagle Vision

Eagle Vision

For those with the fitting genes, there is a new treatment which will enhance your vision to the strength of as a bald eagle. A theory from the 80s which proposed the potential for the transplanting of eagle dna has recently been confirmed as effective.

Expanded Colour Vision

Expanded Colour Vision

There are so many frequencies of electromagnetic spectrum invisible to humans that are visible to creatures such as the Mantis Shrimp. If you are compatible, by 2026 there will be a genetic supplement allowing the user to see up to 6 new colours for multiple hours.

Photographic Memory

Photographic Memory

Tired of studying for tests? Imagine being able to remember anything you’ve seen and call on it instantly. Well the code for photographic memory has been pinpointed and up to ⅓ of the population is estimated to carry the compatible genes.

Enhanced Lucid Dreaming

Enhanced Lucid Dreaming

While not much is known about lucid dreaming, alterations made to a bacteria found on common button cap mushrooms produce a substance that permanently rewires the brain, particularly in redheads, to have more vivid and lucid dreams.

Fast Reaction Time

Fast Reaction Time

The lightning fast reaction and striking types of the king kobra has been isolated to a select few genes which are prominent in those with particular eye colours and skin types. How would you like to be able to move your hands faster than the blink of an eye?

Echolocation

Echolocation

By studying the ears of fruit bats, it is estimated that by 2030 humans will be able to undergo surgery and genetic treatment to see not with light, but by the reflection of sound.

Photosynthesise Sunlight

Photosynthesise Sunlight

Using the genome of blue-green algae, a liquid solution has been developed when when applied to the eyes enables the conversion of sunlight into energy. Scientists have measured 800 kj per day of energy production.

Halve need for sleep

Halve need for sleep

Borrowing the DNA of both sharks and migratory birds, you may only need to sleep for 3 hours per night to get the feeling a full night's rest.

Night vision

Night vision

With the genes of the nocturnal golden possum of Australia, scientists are working on a genetic modification for humans which would allow rapid and ultra-wide dilation of the pupil allowing for night vision superior to any current human.
